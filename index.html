Chuck Schuldiner

The Godfather of death metal
Chuck Schuldiner with his guitar and logo of his band Death

    A Timeline of Chuck Schuldiner's life:

  • 1967 - Born on Long Island, New york.
  • 1976 - Starts playing the guitar, which he gets from his parents, in order to help him grief after his brother's death.
  • 1983 - Schuldiner forms his first band - Mantas, at the age of 16.
  • 1987 - He releases the first album of Death - Scream Bloody Gore.
  • 1989 - 1998 - Every subsequent Death release is different and more complex, making it a staple of death metal.
  • 1999 - Schuldiner is diagnosed with a brain cancer and underwents radiotherapy.
  • 2001 - On December, 13, Schuldiner dies at the age of 34. His memorial service is attended by artists including Dave Grohl, Mike Patton, Jason Newsted and all former members of Death.
